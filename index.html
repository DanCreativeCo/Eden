— Our Story —

Three places. One table.

Eden is built as a small ecosystem: food grown with care, gathered with intention, and served in the middle of town.

What began as a single farm has grown into a small family of places, all rooted in the same belief: that good food starts in good soil, and that a community is fed by more than what's on the plate.

From a working farm just outside town, to a market and eatery on Main Street, to the hands that help other growers do the same — each place is its own, yet they share one philosophy, one source, and one family behind them.
